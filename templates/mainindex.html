Welcome to the Coronavirus Simulation for the NYAS Combating COVID-19 Challenge

This simulation uses data from India to simulate the spread of the novel Coronavirus. Due to computing restrictions, the model has to be limited and therefore is not 100% accurately predicting the outbreak. Nevertheless, it still accurately predicts the spread of the epidemic across India with and without a mask. This simulation will show the use of our mask in combating COVID-19. It checks the increase in number of cases reported from the small-world model. We have also included a secondary button that uses up-to-date data for the current condition of the outbreak in India to establish a control.
